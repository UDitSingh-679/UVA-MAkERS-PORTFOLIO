Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

At UVA Makers, we strive for excellence in every project. However, we understand that circumstances may require cancellation or refund. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be requested and granted for our digital services, including video editing, digital marketing, and web development.

2. Deposit & Payment Structure

Our standard payment structure typically includes:

  • Advance/Deposit: A non-refundable deposit (usually 50% of the project cost) is required before project commencement. This secures your slot in our workflow and covers initial resource allocation.
  • Milestone Payments: For larger projects, payments are tied to deliverables. Refunds for completed milestones are not provided.
  • Final Payment: Due upon project completion. Refunds after final delivery are not applicable.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered under the following conditions:

  • Project Cancellation by Client: If you cancel the project before any work has begun, the advance deposit may be refundable, minus any administrative fees (if applicable) and transaction charges.
  • Project Cancellation by Us: If UVA Makers is unable to commence or complete the project due to unforeseen circumstances (technical issues, force majeure), a full or partial refund will be issued for work not performed.
  • Quality Concerns: If delivered work fails to meet mutually agreed specifications and reasonable revision opportunities do not resolve the issue, a partial refund may be negotiated.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are NOT eligible for refunds:

  • Deposits and advance payments securing project timelines
  • Completed deliverables that meet the agreed specification
  • Work completed beyond the initial proposal scope ("scope creep" work)
  • Third-party expenses incurred (stock footage, music licenses, software subscriptions) purchased specifically for your project
  • Projects discontinued due to client's failure to provide necessary materials, feedback, or approvals in a timely manner

5. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, you must contact us within 7 days of the relevant event (delivery, milestone completion, or project cancellation) via email at uvamakers@gmail.com or WhatsApp at +91 7073669074. Your request should include:

  • Project reference or invoice number
  • Reason for refund request
  • Specific deliverables or milestones in question
  • Any supporting documentation

6. Refund Timeline & Method

If a refund is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7–14 business days from approval.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method (bank transfer, UPI, PayPal, etc.). Processing times vary by payment provider.
  • Transaction fees (if any) may be deducted from the refund amount.

7. Dispute Resolution

In case of disagreement regarding refund eligibility, we encourage direct communication to reach an amicable resolution. If unresolved, disputes shall be subject to arbitration or mediation before resorting to court proceedings, as per the laws of India and jurisdiction of Sikar, Rajasthan courts.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to amend this Refund Policy at any time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Changes apply to requests made after the effective date.
